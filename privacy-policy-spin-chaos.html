Privacy Policy

Spin Chaos

Effective: May 13, 2025 Last updated: May 13, 2025
The short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Spin Chaos works entirely on your device.
01

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. Spin Chaos does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use the app.

All data generated during your use of the app — such as spin counts, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any external server.

02

Data storage

Spin Chaos stores all user preferences and app state exclusively on your local device using standard on-device storage. We do not operate any servers or databases that receive or retain user data.

03

Third-party services

Spin Chaos may use the following third-party services, which may collect certain technical data such as crash reports or anonymous usage statistics according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services — for app distribution and updates
  • Google AdMob — for in-app advertising (if ads are enabled)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services. We have no control over the data collected by third-party SDKs and are not responsible for their practices.

04

Advertising

Spin Chaos may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and other non-personally identifiable information to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings at any time.

05

Children's privacy

Spin Chaos does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any users, no special measures regarding children are necessary beyond this statement.

06

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
