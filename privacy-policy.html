Privacy Policy · com.spinchaosapp

This Privacy Policy describes how Spin Chaos ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Spin Chaos does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers. The app operates primarily offline.

The following data is stored locally on your device only using browser localStorage:

  • Selected spinner and background preferences
  • Sound mode preference
  • Language preference
  • Total spin count (XP)
  • Premium purchase status

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob) 3rd Party

Spin Chaos uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device model and OS version
  • Ad interaction data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

To opt out of personalized advertising, you can adjust your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. In-App Purchases

Spin Chaos offers optional premium purchases (monthly subscription and one-time purchase) via Google Play Billing. All payment transactions are handled exclusively by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

For Google Play's terms, visit: https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/

4. Children's Privacy

Spin Chaos is a general audience app and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data to our servers, there is no risk of a data breach on our end. All locally stored preferences remain on your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

6. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – Advertising
  • Google Play Billing – In-app purchases
  • Capacitor.js – App framework

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date below. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
